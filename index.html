About the Artist
Massachusetts based, ShyAnne Jones, has been honing in her skills while studying Studio Art at Bob Jones University. ShyAnne has blended her love for people and her passion for art. She is open for commissioned work and has plans on creating art during the summer. On her free time, ShyAnne loves to spend quality time with her friends and family, hiking, and of course, doing art!

ShyAnne Jones is a current student at Bob Jones University. She is studying Studio Art. ShyAnne is a talented artist who enjoys using a wide variety of mediums. She specializes in water color portraits and is currently accepting commission work. On the side, ShyAnne likes to spend her free time hiking, and hanging out with friends.
