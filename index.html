All Artwork created by ShyAnne Jones

About the Artist
Massachusetts based, ShyAnne Jones, has been honing in her skills while studying Studio Art at Bob Jones University. ShyAnne has blended her love for people and her passion for art; creating exceptional portraits filled with emotion. She is open for commissioned work and has plans on creating art during the summer. On her free time, ShyAnne loves to spend quality time with her friends and family, hiking, and of course, doing art!
